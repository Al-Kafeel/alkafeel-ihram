Our Story

Serving Pilgrims
with Excellence

Al-Kafeel International is a trusted name in premium Ihram garments, based in Karachi, Pakistan. We supply high-quality Ihram cloth and complete sets to pilgrims across Pakistan and internationally.

Every piece we produce is crafted with care — using the finest unstitched terry and toweling fabric to ensure comfort, absorbency, and full compliance with the sacred requirements of Hajj and Umrah.
